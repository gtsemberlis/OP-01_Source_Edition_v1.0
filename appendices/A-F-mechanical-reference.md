# A-F — Mechanical Reference

## Purpose

Provide a concise operational reference for mechanics that recur during Operation Airspace.

## Recommended Skill Use

| Situation | Suggested Skills |
|---|---|
| Aviation evidence review | Search, Bureaucracy |
| Alexandria technical discussion | Science (Engineering), SIGINT, Computer Science |
| Interviewing witnesses | HUMINT, Persuade |
| Reading Rao's notebooks | Science, Search |
| Collector encounter | Alertness, Firearms, Athletics |
| Runway observation | Alertness, Science |
| Final assessment | Bureaucracy, Persuade |

## Tone Guidance

- Reward observation over interrogation.
- Prefer corroboration over single-point revelation.
- Avoid unnecessary combat.
- Treat competent professionals as competent.
- Preserve uncertainty even after confirmation.

## Optional Rules

- Apply advantage for careful note-taking and comparison of independent evidence.
- Failure should delay or complicate conclusions rather than halt the investigation.

## GM Reminder

Operation Airspace is an investigation about expanding perception, not defeating a monster. Mechanical rulings should reinforce curiosity, professionalism, and evidence-based reasoning.
